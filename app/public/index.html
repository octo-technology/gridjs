Welcome to GridJS!

This website will enable you to compute your
MapReduce calculation in record time by sharing calculating power with the community.

First input your Data. It'll blow your mind.

Give your data as an array. It can be an array of pretty much anything.

Then choose your map function Oh yeah, it's that good.

You choose a calculation formula to be performed on each member of the array, the variable a being the symbol of every entry in the array.

And finally the reduce operation. Checkmate.

Once calculation on every member of the array has been performed, you choose the calculation that will be performed to aggregate your array two by two, using the variables a and b as symbols for two array members next to each other.
